About Me

Hi there! My name is Shaila (Shy-La) and I am currently a junior Web and App Developer at Thomas A. Edison CTE High School. I enjoy writing poetry, producing multimedia content, and eating food!

Among my teammates, I am a UX designer and the team leader. My responsibilities include time management, communication, etc. In the span of a few days, my teammates and I decided upon youth mental health as the main topic of our app.

On the grounds of UX research and ongoing trends of mental health in modern society, SafeGen is designed for youth within the ages of 14 to 21 to strengthen their understanding of mental health and to direct the course their own journey.

I sincerely believe that it's important for young people to access [application] programs that are established by their peers to increase one's willingness to navigate mental health and to minimize the stigma that centers around it. SafeGen strives to be an app that is designed by the youth, for the youth.
