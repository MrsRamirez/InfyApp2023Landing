About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Arisa Moonsammy, a junior at Thomas A Edison Higshchool. I play the designer of our team Sapling. For our app we have decided as a team to focus on youth unemployment. Personally, I think this topic has heavy relevance mainly becuase I am part of youth unemployment. I'm sixteen with no job and I found it important to encourage myself and other people my age, wether it's peers, relatives, or someone I don't know, to benefit themselves with getting employed. Additionally, nowadays I've noticed more people who are my age take interest in wanting a job but struggle because a lot of areas don't hire people who are underaged. We just wanted to present and create an app that people can relate to in a way and to make it eaiser for young adults and teens to find an occupation.
